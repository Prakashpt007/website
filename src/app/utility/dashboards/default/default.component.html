Welcome to CropData

CropData is on a mission to connect millions of distant farmers to knowledge, markets and essential services in a Neutral environment.

Our Mission

CropData is on a mission to impact the lives of the small & marginal farmers, increasing their income and managing their core risks, by providing access to customized knowledge, fair markets, and essential services.

Our Vision

CropData intends to be the leader in the agriculture offtake marketplace for unprocessed crops and focuses on areas that make its model economically viable and self-sustaining.
